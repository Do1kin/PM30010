About Us

MentorMind is an AI-powered career counseling platform designed to help students after 10th grade choose their ideal career path in fields like Medical, Technical, Arts, and Commerce. We combine the power of artificial intelligence with expert insights to guide young minds toward a future filled with purpose and clarity.

Our Vision

To simplify and personalize the career discovery process using AI. We believe that every student has unique potential, and our goal is to help uncover it through smart recommendations and thoughtful career mapping.
